What is the HTML Arcade?

We wanted to recreate the feeling of old flash game websites-in the modern age. With flash being phased out, many of the classic game sites we used to love no longer function. Here, you can relive some of the early 2000s classics, such as Bloons, Bloons Tower Defense 4, and Age of War.
